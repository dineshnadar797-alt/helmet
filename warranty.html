Warranty Information

All HexRide helmets come with a 1-year standard warranty covering manufacturing defects under normal usage conditions.


What is Covered?

  • Shell manufacturing defects
  • Visor mechanism issues
  • Padding or strap defects
  • Paint defects (non-accidental)

What is NOT Covered?

  • Damage from accidents or falls
  • Scratches on visor due to usage
  • Wear and tear from daily use
  • Damage due to improper handling or customization

How to Claim Warranty

Email us at warranty@hexride.com with:


  • Order ID
  • Date of purchase
  • Photos/videos showing the defect

Our support team will verify and respond within 48–72 hours.
