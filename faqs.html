Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


1. Are HexRide helmets ISI certified?


Yes, all HexRide helmets come with ISI certification for maximum safety.


2. How do I find my helmet size?


Measure the circumference of your head above your eyebrows and refer to our size chart.


3. What payment methods do you accept?


We accept COD, UPI, debit/credit cards, and net banking.


4. How long is shipping?


Shipping usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location.


5. Can I return a helmet if the size doesn’t fit?


Yes! Within 7 days of delivery as long as the helmet is unused and in original packaging.
